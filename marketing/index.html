How to Play

1

Join a Match

Enter your name, choose Blitz (2 min) or Standard (5 min) mode, then select Multiplayer or Single Player vs Bots.

2

Start Trading

You begin with $10,000 virtual cash. Buy and sell stocks using the BUY 1/5/10 and SELL 1/5/10 buttons. Watch prices change every 2 seconds.

3

Use Powerups

Select a stock, then activate Insider Info (predictions), Price Freeze (lock price), or Market Pump (force price up). Timing is everything!

4

Win Big

When time runs out, the player with the highest portfolio value (cash + stock holdings) wins. Check the leaderboard for final rankings!
